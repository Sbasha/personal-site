About me


I am a curious individual that is constantly looking to innovate and find better ways to get things done. Coming from a household where my parents came to the country with $200 and no grasp of the English language, I was thrust into a leadership role in the house as the oldest child of 5. My parents not only leveraged their skills into successful careers and ownership of their own house, they raised us to not only become educated, but to use that education to try and benefit those around us. I studied human biology at the University of Toronto for a few years, but I realized that my true passion was in technology and software. Primarily self-taught in all aspects of technology, I also went through NPower Canada which allowed me to sharpen my skills in programming and development. I hope to further refine my skills to become a world-class developer and innovator! I also want to eventually launch multiple applications that not only are successful financially, but also have a positive impact on society. I genuinely want to see everyone succeed, so if you would like to connect whether it is to talk about ideas, or ask questions and find out more, feel free to reach out to me!
